New Forest, Hampshire

Full Day Workshop

  • Cost: £90pp
  • Duration: 8 hrs
  • Group Size: Max 10
  • Included: Transport and lunch

Variety of locations: Woodlands, lakes, rivers, water meadows, heathlands, beach.
Itinerary: We’ll meet at the pick-up location at 9am. The morning will be spent out in the New Forest, followed by lunch back at the studio. The afternoon will be spent editing your photos with Penny on hand to give advice.
Themes can include: Landscapes, Birds, Critters, Flora and Fauna, Ponies, Donkeys, Deer. Landscapes, Vistas, Seascapes.
Seasonal Themes: Purple Heathlands in August. Blaze of colours in Autumn. Fallow deer rutting season in November.
Skills can include: Landscape composition, Lighting, Shutter Speeds. Exposure Triangle. Macro lenses for photographing insects and flowers. Field skills such as building hides to get close up shots of wildlife.

Meet the Photographer

I got my first camera when I was 8 years old. It needed a film and had a little flash. My passion for photography started there. I studied photography and have been a professional photographer for over twenty years.

I enjoy the variety photography offers. I’ve photographed a lot, ranging from weddings, christenings and pets to houseplants, haute couture hats and high end patio stones. My passion though has always been with wildlife, nature and landscape photography.

I enjoy sharing my knowledge of both the New Forest and photography through my wildlife photography workshops. No workshop is ever the same as the landscape, weather and seasons change. If you’d like to learn more about the work I do and about the New Forest head over to my youtube channel. You can see a selection of my photos over on flickr.

Please get in touch here if you’d like to book onto a workshop or for more information.
